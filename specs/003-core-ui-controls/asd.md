**MILF** (Markdown Interface & Live Formatter) is a tiny desktop app that turns plain text into formatted reading on the fly.

## What you're looking at

- The left pane is the **editor** — type or paste markdown there.
- The right pane is the **preview** — it updates as you type.
- Both panes _scroll independently_ once content gets long.

## A quick taste of markdown

You can write [links](https://commonmark.org), drop in some `inline code`, or mix *italic* and **bold** in a sentence.
